Projects

Egyptian Ratscrew

Egyptian Ratscrew is a card game. Similarly to war, the goal of the game is to gather all of the cards in the deck. Players can not see their cards so the only cards visible are on the discard pile. In order for a player to pick up the pile, they can either slap the pile under certain circumstances or win after playing a face card. A player can slap the deck when two cards of the same value are on top of each other, or sandwiching a random card in between them. If a face card is played, then the next player has a pre-determined amount of tries to get another face cards. The amount of tries depends on the face card and are as followed: Jack gets 1 try, Queen gets 2 tries, King gets 3 tries, and Ace gets 4 tries.

Lang

Lang is a programming language. Lang can parse and interpret statements. Lang has typed variables and can handle mathematic operations such as: addition, subtraction, multiplication, and division. All mathematical statements follow PEMDAS rules. Lang can also handle if, for, and while loops. Lang can also handle functions.

Traffic Light Identification

The traffic light identification program uses a neural network to help determine that state of traffic lights are in the direction the car is moving. The program indentifies all lights from left to right. The possible outcomes are a combination of color and direction. The possible colors are red, yellow, and green, and the possible directions are left, straight, or right. This project is meant to be a small portion of a much larger problem, self driving cars.

Reddit Database

This program uses a limited dataset from a sample of Subreddits to populate the data. It is based off of a relational database that can be interacted with using a website. The website not only shows a hand-drawn ER diagram and the tables that make up the databases, but also dives deeper into the dataset. Users will find one special feature for each of the 5 strong entites. For example, a user can search through the most popular subreddits, search for popular posts and give it an award, create a new user, or search for a controversial comment and delete it.
